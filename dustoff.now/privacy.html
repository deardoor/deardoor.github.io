DustOff.Now (the "App") is developed and operated by DearDoor Studio ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App uses and how it is handled. The App helps you temporarily block system input while cleaning your Mac keyboard and provides a solid color screen for display wiping.

1. App overview

The App lets you:

  • Temporarily disable keyboard input to prevent accidental keystrokes.
  • Exit cleaning mode instantly to restore normal typing.
  • Show a solid color screen to make display cleaning easier.

2. Data we collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit personal information. It does not require an account and does not read or record your keystrokes.

3. Local preferences

If you choose any settings (such as a preferred color), they are stored locally on your device. We do not upload this data to any server.

4. Permissions

The App may request macOS Accessibility permission to temporarily block input. This permission is used only for the cleaning mode and not to monitor or log your activity.

5. Analytics, ads, and third-party services

The App does not include advertising and does not integrate third-party analytics SDKs.

6. Data retention and deletion

Any local preferences remain on your device until you delete the App. You can remove the App at any time to clear all local data.

7. Children's privacy

The App is not intended for children. If local laws define a minimum age (for example, under 13), those requirements apply.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the App or legal requirements. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated accordingly.

9. Contact
