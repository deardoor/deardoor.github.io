Tomato-Mac (the "App") is developed and operated by DearDoor Studio ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App uses and how it is handled. The App is a macOS menu bar Pomodoro timer with white noise and statistics.

1. App overview

The App lets you:

  • Start and stop Pomodoro sessions from the macOS status bar.
  • Customize durations, names, and defaults for focus and break cycles.
  • Play white noise with volume control and sound selection.
  • Review today’s sessions and historical statistics.

2. Data you provide

The App stores the settings you create, such as Pomodoro durations, labels, default selections, white noise preferences, and auto-play choices. The App also generates session statistics based on your usage. No account or sign-in is required.

3. Local storage

All settings and statistics are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. We do not transmit your data to our servers. Depending on your system settings, this data may be included in macOS backups (such as iCloud). Those backups are managed by your operating system or service provider.

4. Analytics, ads, and third-party services

The current version of the App does not include in-app ads and does not integrate third-party analytics SDKs that track you across apps or websites.

If this changes in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy to explain what data is collected, how it is used, and how you can manage it.

5. Permissions

The App may access audio output to play white noise. It does not access your contacts, photos, location, camera, or microphone.

6. Data retention and deletion

Your data remains on your device while the App is installed. You can reset your settings within the App or delete the App to remove all local data.

7. Children's privacy

The App is not intended for children. If local laws define a minimum age (for example, under 13), those requirements apply.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or legal requirements. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated, and we may also notify you within the App when appropriate.

9. Contact

If you have any questions, feedback, or privacy-related requests about Tomato-Mac, you can contact us at:

Email: support@madeincoder.com
