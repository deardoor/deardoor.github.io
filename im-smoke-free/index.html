The Contract Model
  • Permanent Unlock (The Last Pack). One-time purchase, roughly equal to the cost of a single pack of cigarettes in your region. Let this be the last tobacco-related expense of your life.
  • The Discipline (One Single Cigarette). If you break your promise—even for a single puff—resetting your timer is designed to carry a small but meaningful cost (for example, the price of one cigarette or a short reflective ad). This minor friction is a psychological barrier that keeps you on the path when your willpower wavers.
  • No Gradual Reduction. I'm Smoke-Free is built around one principle: stop giving your addiction exit routes. No tapering illusions, no “maybe later”. Just a clear, honest pact with yourself.
